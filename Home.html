ChocCreator Pty Ltd

About

ChocCreator was launched in October 2012 and has since been providing consultancy services to the confectionery industry, both within Australia and Internationally.

Based in Sydney, we have the resources to assist your business to achieve its objectives, from the "Where do I start?" questions through to streamlining production processes.

With over 35 years of Confectionery experience, Mark Tubman leads a team ready to assist you in your development requirements.

Consultancy Services

Consultancy Services Include:

  • Product concept feasibilities and appraisals
  • Product and formulation development
  • Ingredient and equipment sourcing
  • Laboratory and production trials
  • Production line commissioning
  • Trouble shooting and problem solving
  • Staff training in confectionery technology
  • Production line filming
